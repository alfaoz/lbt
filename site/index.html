how it prices

Match the real market

Recent sold auctions of the same item, same displayed tier, same recomb state — pets also level-banded and candy-matched. Falls back gracefully (with a visible warning) when a market is thin.

Normalize every comparable

Each sale's own upgrades are priced off the bazaar and subtracted, leaving a distribution of what the bare item trades for. Outliers are fenced off.

Rebuild for your item

A conservative percentile of that base, plus your item's parts at resale-realistic haircuts, blended with — and capped by — the live ask wall for your build.

Offer with a margin

The lowball is fair value minus a liquidity-based discount you can nudge live, and never above what the seller would net just listing it themselves.
